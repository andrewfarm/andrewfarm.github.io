boids

A real-time simulation of fish-like creatures schooling. An immersive, yet minimalistic world of predator and prey, interactive plants, and realistic behavior based on the original Boids algorithm by Craig Reynolds.

visualizer

A simple but elegant visualizer for audio, which also includes a real-time spectrogram. I'd like to use the spectrogram data to automatically transcribe music, so we'll see how that goes?

electrocity

A real-time simulation (god, another one?) of electrically charged objects interacting. This was the first physical simulation I wrote, and comes with visual features such as field lines (shown here), field-tracing particles and color map.

Circle Invasion!

A classic arcade-style game. A square, who must fight against the advancing circles. Why does he fight? He does not know. There is no other way. There is only the crippling ennui that shrouds his limited existence. With each wave the circles come stronger and with an increased will to live, something which will be torn from them only seconds later. So has it been, and so will it continue, as ammunition depletes and the square and circle draw nearer both to oblivion. The square screams.
